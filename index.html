Services & methods

What I do

Answers you can act on, and defend

Most analysts hand back numbers. I deliver the tables, figures, and plain-language interpretation that say what your data actually means, ready for a manuscript, a board report, or your next decision, and built to hold up when someone pushes back.

Study & survey design

The right design, sample size, and instruments, chosen before you collect.

Group comparisons & intervention analysis

Pre/post and between-group tests (t-tests, ANOVA, Wilcoxon, Mann–Whitney) with effect sizes, not just p-values.

SEM & latent-variable analysis

Measurement models, multi-group comparison, and relationships between constructs.

Psychometric & scale analysis

Reliability, validity, and how Likert/ordinal instruments behave across groups.

Regression & predictive modeling

Linear, logistic, and mixed/multilevel models for nested or repeated-measures data.

Deep statistical analysis

The heavy lifting behind the visuals: robust methods, assumption checks, and messy or non-standard data handled honestly.

Data pipelines & automation

Clean data in, refreshed reports out. Pipelines and backend infrastructure that keep working long after the project ends.

Working together

How it works

  1. We talk, free of charge

    A short call about your question and your data.

  2. You get an honest read

    What the data can and can't support. If simpler is right, I'll say so.

  3. Your data stays protected

    De-identified wherever possible, under a confidentiality agreement.

  4. You get something usable

    Analysis, tables, figures, and an interpretation you can defend.

Data confidentiality

Research data is sensitive, and I treat it that way: a signed confidentiality agreement before I see anything, de-identified or aggregated data wherever the analysis allows, and secure handling only for the agreed purpose. If your work involves a research ethics board, I'll work within its requirements.
