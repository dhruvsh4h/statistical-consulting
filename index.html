Services & methods

What I do

Results, written for your paper

Analysis delivered with the tables, effect sizes, and plain-language interpretation you can put straight into a manuscript, thesis, or grant report — and defend in review. Most statisticians hand back numbers; I hand back something ready to write up.

The difference

Study & survey design

Choosing the right design, sample size, and instruments before you collect data — so the analysis is sound from the start, not rescued afterward.

Group comparisons & intervention analysis

Pre/post and between-group evaluation using the appropriate parametric or non-parametric tests (t-tests, ANOVA, Wilcoxon signed-rank, Mann–Whitney), with effect sizes — not just p-values.

Structural Equation Modeling (SEM) & latent-variable analysis

Measurement models, multi-group comparison, and modeling relationships between constructs for psychometric and survey research.

Psychometric & scale analysis

Reliability, validity, and the behaviour of Likert/ordinal instruments across groups.

Regression & predictive modeling

Linear, logistic, and mixed/multilevel models for nested or repeated-measures data.

Working together

How it works

  1. We talk — free of charge

    We start with a short call about your research question and your data. No charge to scope it.

  2. You get an honest read

    I tell you what the data can and can't support. If a simpler approach is right, I'll say so.

  3. Your data stays protected

    I work with de-identified data wherever possible, on your terms, under a confidentiality agreement.

  4. You get something you can use

    The analysis, the tables and figures, and a plain-language interpretation you can write up and defend.

Data confidentiality

Research data is sensitive, and I treat it that way. I'll sign a confidentiality agreement before seeing anything, work with de-identified or aggregated data wherever the analysis allows, and handle all data securely and only for the agreed purpose. If your work involves a research ethics board, I'm happy to work within its requirements.
